NotesHQ / Careers

Working at NotesHQ.

We're a small team building NotesHQ slowly and carefully — the same way you'd hope your second brain was built.

No open positions

We're not hiring right now.

There are no vacancies at this time. NotesHQ is being built by a small, deliberate team, and we only open roles when we know exactly who we need and why. Please check back here in future — or stay in touch below.

How we work.

Remote-first, asynchronous by default, with deep-work blocks protected by a shared calendar. We ship in small, finished increments rather than ambitious roadmaps that slip. Quality of judgment matters more than years of experience.

Who we hire.

People who care about craft, write clearly, and can carry a problem from "vague idea" to "working in production" with very little hand-holding. We hire generalists slightly more often than specialists, and we don't ask anyone to interview through a take-home longer than two hours.

Want to stay in touch?

If you'd like to introduce yourself for future consideration, send us a short note and a link or two. We keep a small file of speculative applications and reach out first when a relevant role opens.
