How it works

One button. Four moments.

Pebble is designed for the half-second between "I just thought of something" and "wait, where did it go?"

01

Press once

A gentle haptic confirms recording started. The button lights warm orange. No screen needed.

02

Say the thing

Talk like you'd talk to a friend. Background noise filtered with three on-device beam-forming mics.

03

Sync on the way home

When you're in Bluetooth range of your phone, Pebble uploads the audio and the transcript appears in your notes.

04

Ask NoteChat

Your Pebble recordings are searchable like any other note. Ask "what did I think about on Tuesday's run?"

Common questions

The things people ask before ordering.

When does Pebble ship? +

We're shipping the first batch in May 2026, in the order pre-orders were placed. You'll get an email with a tracking number two days before dispatch.

Do I need a NotesHQ account? +

Yes — Pebble is a companion device. Recording works offline on the device, but transcripts and search live in the app. The free NotesHQ account is enough; Premium is not required.

Where does the audio go? +

Recorded audio is encrypted on the device with a key only your account holds. It uploads to NotesHQ over Bluetooth or Wi-Fi when in range of your phone, is transcribed, and is then stored encrypted on our EU servers. Your audio is never used to train AI models.

Can I return it? +

Yes — 14 days from delivery for a full refund, provided the device is in re-sellable condition. Email orders@noteshq.ai and we'll send a pre-paid return label.

Does it work without the phone? +

It records without the phone. The 32 GB on-device storage holds about 480 hours of audio. Transcription and search happen when it next syncs.

Is there a bulk discount for teams? +

Yes — 10+ units gets 15% off, 25+ gets 25% off. Write to orders@noteshq.ai with your order size and we'll send a quote.
