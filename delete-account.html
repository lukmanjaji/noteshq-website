Delete your NotesHQ account.

Your account, your call. Here's how to permanently remove your NotesHQ account and everything stored alongside it.

How to request deletion.

NotesHQ honors deletion requests within 30 days. We accept requests through either route below — both reach the same team, both produce the same outcome.

Option 1 — From the app (fastest).

On any device where you're signed in:

  1. Open NotesHQ.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm with your password.

Your account is queued for permanent deletion immediately. Confirmation lands in your inbox within a minute.

Option 2 — Email request.

If you can no longer sign in or prefer not to use the app, email us:

zshift@zshift.io

Subject: Delete my NotesHQ account. Send the email from the address tied to your account so we can verify. We'll reply once the deletion is complete (within 30 days, usually within 48 hours).

What gets deleted.

When you request account deletion, the following is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days:

  • Your profile. Email, display name, country, password hash, OAuth links (Google / Apple), avatar, daily word goal.
  • All your notes. Text notes, freehand sketches, voice memos and their transcripts, checklist items, uploaded images and files.
  • Notebooks and tags. Including nested sub-notebooks, color labels, and any organization metadata.
  • Reminders and writing-streak data. Including scheduled push notifications, recurring reminder rules, and daily word-count history.
  • Vault contents. Encrypted notes inside your vault are erased along with the encryption metadata.
  • Public share links. Any active /share/... URLs you've minted stop working immediately and are deleted with the rest of your data.
  • Support tickets. Open and historical support conversations you've had with us.
  • AI history. Past NoteChat conversations and quiz attempts.
  • Subscription data. Your Stripe / RevenueCat customer ID is anonymized in our system; the corresponding payment-provider records are subject to that provider's own retention rules.

What is retained, and why.

A small amount of data is kept after account deletion. Each item below has a legitimate reason — typically a legal or fraud-prevention obligation.

  • Billing records (invoices, payment confirmations, refunds): retained for 6 years, as required by UK tax law (HMRC). These records contain transaction metadata only — no note content.
  • Audit and security logs (sign-in events, IP addresses tied to authentication): retained for up to 90 days to detect abuse and respond to security incidents. After 90 days they are anonymized.
  • Anonymized analytics: aggregate event counts ("a user created a note") with no link back to your account. Retained indefinitely. We never recombine these with personal data.
  • Backups: encrypted database backups roll off our backup storage within 30 days. Until that window expires, your data may exist in a non-restorable form on backup volumes; it is overwritten and cannot be re-associated with an account after the window closes.

Timeline.

What to expect after you ask.

  • Within minutes — your account is signed out everywhere and your notes stop syncing.
  • Within 48 hours — your account row, notes, files, and active sessions are erased from production storage.
  • Within 30 days — backup tapes containing your data roll off; no copy of your content remains.
  • After 90 days — security logs are anonymized; no record of your IP addresses remains.

Want a copy first?

Account deletion is permanent — there's no undo. If you want to keep what you wrote, export your notes before requesting deletion:

  • On the web: Settings → Data → Export all notes. You'll get a ZIP containing every note as Markdown plus your uploaded images and audio.
  • On mobile: open any note → share icon → Save as PDF (per-note); or use the web app for the bulk export.

Questions before you decide.

If you're considering deletion because something isn't working — a stuck sync, a wrong charge, missing notes — we'd rather fix it. Email zshift@zshift.io first. If it's still not working, we'll process the deletion for you — no friction.
