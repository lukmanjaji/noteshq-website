Good questions

Frequently asked.

Is the free version really free? +

Yes — £0, no credit card, no time limit, no feature throttling. The free plan covers unlimited notes, sketches, voice memos, notebooks, vault, and sync forever. Only the AI features (NoteChat & NoteQ) require Premium.

What does Premium add? +

Unlimited NoteChat (chat with your notes) and NoteQ (AI-generated quizzes from your notes and uploaded documents), plus priority sync, faster transcription, AI-powered summaries, and priority support.

Is my data private? +

Yes. Your notes are encrypted in transit and at rest. Vault notes use end-to-end encryption — we cannot decrypt them. We never sell data and never train AI models on your content.

Does it work offline? +

Yes. Take notes offline; they'll sync to all your devices the moment you reconnect. AI features (NoteChat & NoteQ) require an internet connection.

What platforms are supported? +

iOS, Android, and the web. All three share the same data, same vault, same notebooks.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. Cancel from Settings, no questions asked. You keep all your notes; AI features simply pause. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans.
