Calendar integration

Lives where your schedule already does.

Every NotesHQ reminder and checklist deadline syncs into the calendar app you already use. One URL, set it once, refreshed automatically. No browser extensions, no OAuth dance, no second app to open.

Google Calendar

Settings → Add calendar → From URL → paste your NotesHQ URL. Updates every few hours.

Apple Calendar

Tap "Open in calendar" from NotesHQ — iOS and macOS handle the rest. Works on iPad too.

Outlook

Calendar → Add calendar → Subscribe from web → paste the URL. Works in Outlook 365, desktop, and on the web.

Thunderbird

File → New → Calendar → On the Network → iCalendar (ICS). Free, open source, runs on Linux too.

Anything else that speaks iCalendar (RFC 5545) works too — Fastmail, ProtonMail, Nextcloud, Linux clients, calendar widgets on smartwatches. One URL, every device.

And the daily review lands in every inbox

Opt in to a short morning email — today's reminders, anything overdue, and your writing streak — delivered at the hour you pick, in your local timezone. Same trusted pipeline as account emails: sent from zshift.io, SPF + DKIM signed, clean delivery to Gmail, Outlook, ProtonMail, iCloud, Yahoo, Fastmail, and any other RFC-compliant inbox.

Browser clipper

Save any webpage in one click.

Reading something worth keeping? Hit the toolbar button — NotesHQ captures the page's URL, title, and any text you'd selected, then drops it into your library. Use the keyboard shortcut Alt + Shift + S if you'd rather not reach for the mouse.

Good questions

Frequently asked.

Is the free version really free? +

Yes — £0, no credit card, no time limit. Basic gives you 50 notes (20 text + 10 canvas), 5 notebooks, 1-minute voice memos with 30 minutes of free monthly transcription, voice transcription in 25 languages with auto-detect, note translation across 24 languages (first 1,000 words per note), plus sync, vault, and the full canvas. Upgrade to Premium when you outgrow the caps or want the AI features.

What does Premium add? +

Unlimited notes & notebooks, longer voice memos with unlimited transcription, NoteChat (ask your notes anything), NoteQ (auto-generated quizzes), AI summaries, smart tag suggestions, tables, linked notes, the focus timer, recurring reminders, version-history diff, and phone & email priority support.

Is my data private? +

Yes. Your notes are encrypted in transit and at rest. Vault notes use end-to-end encryption — we cannot decrypt them. We never sell data and never train AI models on your content.

Does it work offline? +

Yes. Take notes offline; they'll sync to all your devices the moment you reconnect. AI features (NoteChat & NoteQ) require an internet connection.

What platforms are supported? +

The web app (works in any modern browser), Android — get it on Google Play — and iOS / iPadOS — download on the App Store. All three share the same data, vault, and notebooks. There's also a browser clipper for Chrome / Edge / Firefox / Brave / Vivaldi / Opera (see Clipper above).

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. Cancel from Settings, no questions asked. You keep all your notes; AI features simply pause. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans.
